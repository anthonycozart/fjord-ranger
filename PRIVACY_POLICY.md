# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 2026_

Fjord Ranger is a personal hobby project. It is not a business.

## What we collect

- Your phone number, which you provide by texting the signup keyword.
- Your slot preferences.
- Records of notifications sent to you.

## How we use it

Your phone number is used solely to send you SMS notifications about available sauna slots, and to receive your replies. It is never sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.

## Data storage

Your data is stored in a private database. It is not shared with any third party except Twilio, which delivers the SMS messages.

## Opting out

Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages and have your number removed.

## Contact

This is a hobby project with no formal support channel. If you have concerns, contact the person who invited you.
